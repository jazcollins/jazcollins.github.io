Research

My current research interests include computer vision and human perception. I'm interested in how we can teach computers to see and perceive the world as a collection of objects in 3D without requiring hundreds of thousands of annotations. Plus, how can computational tools be used in novel ways to help us better understand and uncover insights about human perception?
